How to register for classes:
"myGateway" is a multi-purpose student web portal though which all students attending Fullerton College, Cypress College and/or the School of Continuing Education can manage most things related to being a student in the North Orange County Community College District.  From each student's myGateway account, he or she will be able to enroll in classes, drop classes, view grades and unofficial transcripts as well as a host of other things.

After applying to the college, students will receive an email from the Admissions and Records office with their student ID # and instructions for logging into their personal myGateway account.

To access your myGateway account, you must use your 6-digit birth date (MMDDYY) and either your (8-digit) student ID # or your Social Security #. 

Registration appointment information (i.e. date and time) will also be sent out through myGateway.  To view your appointment date and time, log into your myGateway account and click on the "Student" tab.

All students are required to register for classes through the myGateway web portal. 

If you're having difficulty with your PIN, you may need to reset it.  To reset your PIN, go to the myGateway website, then click on the link "PIN Reset".

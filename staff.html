Latest News

Students interested in transfer to the California State University system (CSU) now have another pathway to facilitate their transition from the Community College to the CSU - the AA-T and AS-T degrees.  Currently, Fullerton College is offering four 
AA-T/AS-T degrees, with more on the way...

For additional information about these transfer/degree options at Fullerton College, please visit the FC Transfer Center or Transfer Center website.
